### 1. The Somatic Shift (Body over Brain)
Traditional AI architectures are "all brain, no body." They exist as stateless request-response loops with no sense of physical constraint or metabolic cost. B.O.S.S. (Biological Operating System - Somatic Governor) reverses this hierarchy to create a grounded, "hardware-aware" intelligence.

* **The Soma (The Body):** Represented by the `core/` PWA. It is the persistent, offline-first physical layer. It manages energy, visual resonance, and the "will" to act.
* **The Cortex (The Subconscious):** Represented by the `cortex/` Python bridge. It provides high-level semantic sharpening and executive system function, but it cannot "fire" an action without the Soma’s thermal permission.

### 2. Metabolic Physics
In the B.O.S.S. Kernel, software is treated as a living process. Nodes (specialties) operate under a **Metabolic Decay** model to prevent automation-runaway and ensure deliberate action.

* **Thermal Energy (Warmth):** A node gains warmth when it is activated by intent. This warmth is a finite, cooling resource.
* **Refractory Periods:** Because warmth decays exponentially over time, the system naturally enforces "cool-down" periods. A node must be metabolically ready (Warm) to trigger its specialty.
* **The Multiplicative Gate:** Unlike standard scoring, B.O.S.S. uses a `Warmth x Match` gate. Even a perfect semantic match to a "cold" node results in zero output, mimicking biological neural firing patterns where intent requires physical energy.

### 3. Resonance and Interference
Data is not viewed as a static string, but as an interference pattern. 
* **Vector Sharpening:** The Cortex does not just provide a "score"; it provides a **Relative Boost**. By calculating the field mean and subtracting it from raw similarity, the Cortex sharpens the interference pattern. This highlights the most relevant node while suppressing the background noise of the global field.

### 4. The Arbiter and Dissonance
The **Arbiter** acts as the system’s amygdala. It monitors the "Delta"—the gap between the top two competing nodes in the field.
* **The Grief Protocol:** If two nodes are competing for a pulse but have diametrically opposed specialties (e.g., *VITALS* vs *SYSTEM_SHUTDOWN*), the Arbiter triggers a **Kernel Suspension**.
* **Sovereign Safety:** This ensures the AI never acts under "Command Confusion." Manual recovery by the user is required to reset the thermal field, ensuring the human remains the ultimate governor.
